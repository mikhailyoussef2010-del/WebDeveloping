Minecraft is a popular sandbox game developed by Mojang. It allows players to explore a blocky world, gather resources, and build structures freely. The game offers different modes, such as Survival and Creative, giving players various ways to enjoy it. With its simple graphics and endless possibilities, Minecraft encourages creativity, problem-solving, and exploration. It has become one of the best-selling games in the world and is loved by players of all ages.
